CSC Advising - Class of 2025

Course registration time for Spring 2024 is almost here! Your individual registration time is listed in your myNova student account (under "My Schedule and Registration").

Please sign up to meet with one of your advisors (Dr. Mirela Damian, Dr. Justin DeBenedetto and Dr. Ebel Nwafor) to discuss your course selection and get your registration PIN. Prepare for your meeting by researching the Master Schedule of Classes (or using the Nova Schedule Builder available in myNova ) and bring your proposed schedule to the meeting. Consult the sample schedule available under "Current Student Resources" on the CSC website to guide your course selection.
