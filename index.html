CSC Advising - Class of 2029

Congratulations, first-year advisees! You’re nearly through your first semester at Villanova. It's time for your Spring 2026 registration. Your individual registration time is listed in your myNova student account (under "My Schedule and Registration").

Your advising team this semester includes Dr. Nathaniel Chodosh, Dr. Mirela Damian, Dr. Justin DeBenedetto, and Dr. Ebel Nwafor. Faculty advisors work in teams to ensure that students always have access to an advisor, even when one member is unavailable.

Please sign up to meet with one of your advisors to discuss your course selection and obtain your registration PIN. Before your meeting, review the Master Schedule of Classes or use the Nova Schedule Builder in myNova, and bring a draft of your proposed schedule to the meeting. You should also consult the sample schedule under “Current Student Resources” on the Computing Sciences website to help guide your course selection.
